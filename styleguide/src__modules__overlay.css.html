Overlay

  • stylesheet
src/modules/overlay.css  

Overlays can be used to block the app or parts of it, for example while a modal is open or during loading.

Only styles are provided, create functionality with JS. See the JS tab for an example on how to display/hide the overlay.

.oph-overlay-is-visible element's z-index may have to overridden depending on your app.
